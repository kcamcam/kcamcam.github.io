Exam Bank Spring 2015

Exambank is a website providing study materials for students in Engineering, Software Engineering and Computer Science at Concordia. The project was created as a means to pay it forward, give back to the community and to provide quality study material for future students. It started from humble beginings to today reaching about 2 thousand students per month during the regular the semester and generating roughly 10 thousand page views per month during exam periods. A pretty simple JS script helps to easily update and maintain contributions from other students more effectively without having to pay for server space.
